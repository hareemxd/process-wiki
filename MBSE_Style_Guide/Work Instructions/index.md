## Summary of Work Instructions

This section of the style guide contains *Work Instructions*.

### Purpose

The purpose of a Work Instruction (WI) is to provide specific, detailed guidance to employees in performing their work tasks. Common elements of Work Instructions include:  
* using specific tools and platforms  
* step-by-step instructions with visual aids
* references to other documents to manage document scope 


<ac:structured-macro ac:name="info">
  <ac:rich-text-body>
    <p>Work Instructions demonstrate a specific way to perform a task, but there are other ways that are acceptable as long as they comply with the applicable procedures and processes.</p>
  </ac:rich-text-body>
</ac:structured-macro>

#### Process Hierarchy


|Level|Genre|Purpose|
|---|---|---|
|1|Process|What needs to be done, and why.|
|2|Procedure| How the process needs to be done.
|3|Work Instruction| How to do the procedure.|
